line 1: Give me the strength lightly to bear my joys and sorrows.
line 2: Give me the strength to make my love fruitful in service.
line 3: Give me the strength never to disown the poor or bend my knees before insolent might.
line 4: Give me the strength to raise my mind high above daily trifles.
line 5: And give me the strength to surrender my strength to thy will with love.
